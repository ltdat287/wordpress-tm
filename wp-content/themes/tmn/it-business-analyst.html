Vị trí: IT BUSINESS ANALYST

Apply

Hà Nội  -  Full Time

Số lượng : 2

Mô tả công việc:

  • Elicit business and system requirements from client
  • Assist client in defining requirements and suggest ideas
  • Create SRS (System Requirement Spec), Feature List, UC list and UC detail, GBR (General Business Rules), wire frame for complex UCs
  • Work with UI for UI design and screen mock up
  • Reviewing and providing the appropriate feedback on the final implementation of the user interface
  • Assist Project Manager on project scope, estimation and planning
  • Assist Project Manager on requirement sign off process and managing project change requests
  • Acting as the bridge between client and offshore to clarify requirement with development team (developers, QC, UI designer) both business and system analysis view as well as the bridge among internal team
  • Work with QC to verify that all requirements of the system are fulfilled by the implementation in the traceability manner among requirements, CR and implementation
  • Support UAT in testing, verifying bugs/issues with client

Yêu cầu:

  • At least 1 years of BA experience in Software Development for enterprise business
  • Experience in UML design, data modeling
  • Have knowledge and experience in Enterprise Software Development
  • Nice to have SQL knowledge
  • Proven ability in typical BA skills set: requirements engineering, business process analysis, modelling techniques
  • Excellent command of English skills (Writing, Speaking, Listening, Reading)

Bạn nên gửi kèm theo cho chúng tôi:

  • CV và hồ sơ cá nhân
  • Bất kỳ thông tin nào khác mà bạn nghĩ rằng sẽ là hữu ích nhất đối với chúng tôi trong việc tìm hiểu thêm về bạn
  • Ưu tiên hồ sơ theo mẫu văn bản.
